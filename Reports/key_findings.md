# Key Findings

## Revenue & Growth
- $15.42M total revenue across 96,478 delivered orders
- $159.83 average order value
- November 2017 was the highest revenue month (Black Friday)
- São Paulo state drives 37% of total revenue

## Customer Satisfaction
- Average review score: 4.09/5
- 92.1% on-time delivery rate
- Orders 8+ days late average just 1.70★ 
  — a 2.59-point drop from on-time orders

## Sellers
- 3,095 active sellers
- Top 10% of sellers generate 67% of revenue
- Only 3.1% of customers are repeat buyers

## Recommendations
1. Investigate causes of 8+ day delivery delays
2. Build a retention program for top-performing sellers
3. Treat customer retention as separate from acquisition
4. Align seller recruitment with high-demand categories
